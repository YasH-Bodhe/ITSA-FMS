Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register for an event? +
To register for an event, navigate to the Events section on our homepage, click on the event you're interested in, and follow the registration instructions. You'll need to be logged in to complete the registration process.
Can I participate in multiple events? +
Yes! You can register and participate in as many events as you'd like, provided there are no scheduling conflicts. Check the event details for dates and times to plan accordingly.
How can I cancel my registration? +
To cancel a registration, go to your Profile page and view your registered events. There you'll find an option to cancel your registration. Please note that some events may have cancellation deadlines.
Are there any registration fees? +
Registration fees vary by event. Free events are clearly marked, while others may have a nominal fee. The fee details are provided on each event's registration page.
What types of technical events are offered? +
Our technical events include hackathons, coding competitions, robotics challenges, AI/ML workshops, technical paper presentations, and more. These events are designed to test and enhance your technical skills.
Do I need prior experience for non-technical events? +
Most non-technical events are designed to be accessible to all participants regardless of experience level. Each event description will specify if any prerequisites or experience is recommended.
